How to Use Deaf Mode

This mode helps people who are deaf or hard of hearing by converting speech to text and text to speech with multiple language support.

Features

  • Real-time speech recognition
  • Text-to-speech conversion
  • Multiple language support (English, Hindi, Marathi)
  • Easy language switching
  • Copy to clipboard functionality

Tips for Best Results

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Reduce background noise when using microphone
  • Use punctuation in text for better speech synthesis
  • Keep the microphone close to your mouth

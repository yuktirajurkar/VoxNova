How to Use Mute Mode

This mode helps people who are mute or have speech difficulties by recognizing sign language and converting it to text.

Features

  • Real-time sign language recognition
  • Multiple sign support
  • Copy to clipboard functionality
  • Speech synthesis for feedback

Tips for Best Results

  • Ensure good lighting conditions
  • Keep your hands visible to the camera
  • Make clear and distinct signs
  • Position yourself at a reasonable distance from the camera

Supported Signs

  • Hello
  • Thank You
  • Yes/No
  • Goodbye
  • Help
  • Please
  • Sorry
  • Water
